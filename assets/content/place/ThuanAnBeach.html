Thuan An Beach

Thuan An Beach is a beach in Thua Thien-Hue, Vietnam. Hue Beach 15 km to the east. This is a famous tourist place of this province. Thuan An beach of Thuan An Town, Phu Vang district. Thuan An Beach is located next to Thuan An estuary, where the river then poured into the Tam Giang lagoon to the sea. The beach city. Busiest period here lasts from April to September when the weather is hottest.
