Tu Duc Tomb

Tu Duc tomb is a complex of structures, including the burial place of King Tu Duc is located in a narrow valley of Duong Xuan Thuong village, the chief of the total Cu (old), this is the Upper Three Village, Thuy Xuan Ward Hue City. At the new building, the mausoleum called the Van Nien, then run with rebellion, went renamed Tu Duc Khiem Cung. After losing Tu Duc Tomb, renamed Khiem Lang. Tu Duc Tomb architecturally sophisticated, charming scenery and is one of the most beautiful tombs of Nguyen kings.
