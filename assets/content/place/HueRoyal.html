Hue Royal

Hue Imperial City in Hue ancient citadel , where the capital of the Nguyen dynasty during the 140 years from 1805 to 1945 . Currently Hue Citadel is a relic of the cluster relics of Hue was recognized by UNESCO as World Cultural Heritage . Hue Citadel by King Gia Long from the survey conducted in 1803 , started construction since 1805 and completed in 1832 under King Minh Mang . Currently, Hue Citadel is located in Hue maps as follows : the south Tran Hung Dao and Le Duan , Le Duan Street to the west , the north border Tang Bat Ho street , east Phan Dang Luu . Inside the city , is limited to under the road map as follows : south Ong Ich Khiem street , west Ton cards ; roads north and east Luong Ngoc Quyen street is Spring 68 .
