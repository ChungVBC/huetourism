Nam Giao

Dan Nam Giao Nguyen Dynasty is where the Nguyen emperors heaven worship ceremony held in the spring every year , located in Chang'an ward , city Hue . This is the only forum Nam Giao exists ( though not in intact condition ) in Vietnam , as well as the only international forum exists among many men an ancient world . Nam Giao Festival is the most important ritual under the monarchy because the king only has the authority to make sacrifices Communications , ie economic drift , to confirm the legitimacy of the dynasty , the emperor 's authority compliance under the mandate of heaven that we govern . Thus , almost all Vietnam feudal dynasties were held ceremony for the construction of Communications and Transportation Forum South . Also to that end , the Forum Nam Giao Nguyen has started construction on March 25, 1806 . Once completed , King Gia Long was first held in Giao ceremony here on March 27, 1807 .
